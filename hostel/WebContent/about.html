About SES Boys Hostel

Undertaking

1. I am aware that allocation of room will be made by the Rector on a First-Come-First-Served basis.

2. If I am found indulging in anti-social activities, creating nuisance to fellow inmates, or any other mischief, I will be expelled from the hostel. The fees will not be refundable in such cases.

3. During college sessions, I should not be found in the hostel, otherwise, I shall be liable for strict disciplinary action.

4. In case I want leave(s) for some days, I should obtain prior written permission from the Rector/Principal. I am aware that absence from the hostel without prior written permission on the prescribed form from the warden may even lead to expulsion from the hostel.

5. I am aware that if any magazine, poster, or literature with objectionable pictures is found pasted on the walls of the room/doors/windows/premises of the hostel, it will lead to my expulsion from the hostel.

Anti-Ragging Undertaking

I have been admitted to SES BOYS' HOSTEL and received a copy of the AICTE regulations on curbing the menace of ragging in higher educational institutions, 2009 (hereinafter called the "Regulations"), and read & fully understood the provisions contained in the said Regulations.

I have, in particular, perused Clause 3 of the Regulations and am aware of what constitutes ragging.

I have also, in particular, perused Clause 7 and Clause 9.1 of the Regulations and am fully aware of the penal and administrative action that is liable to be taken against me in case I am found guilty of or abetting ragging, actively or passively, or being part of a conspiracy to promote ragging.

I hereby solemnly aver and undertake that:

  • I will not indulge in any behavior or act that may be constituted as ragging under Clause 3 of the Regulations.
  • I will not participate in or abet or propagate through any act of commission or omission that may be constituted as ragging under Clause 3 of the Regulations.
  • I will report immediately any incident of ragging that comes to my notice.
  • I understand that any violation of this undertaking will lead to appropriate disciplinary action.
